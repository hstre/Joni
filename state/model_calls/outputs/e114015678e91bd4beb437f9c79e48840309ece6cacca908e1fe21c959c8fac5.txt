  "AC-ODM approaches data mixing from a reinforcement learning perspective with a parameterized policy that acts as a dynamic linear surrogate maximizing the constructive interference of gradients.",
  "AC-ODM supports two operational modes: a proxy mode for fixed, pre-prepared corpora with policy transfer from a small model to a larger target, and a non-proxy mode for direct end-to-end training from scratch without priors.",
  "Empirically, AC-ODM significantly outperforms prior methods in convergence speed and downstream accuracy, with up to 66% fewer training steps, 27.5% relative improvement in MMLU, and 2.23x higher pass@1 on HumanEval, while incurring 0.4% per-step wall-clock increase and 2% memory overhead."
