["We introduce PhySciBench, a benchmark highly relevant to physical science research, comprising 200 expert-curated questions, balanced between physics and chemistry, across six task categories.", "Evaluations reveal even the strongest baseline, Gemini Deep Research, achieves only 33.5% accuracy; failure analysis identifies fragility in extended reasoning chains, limited knowledge transfer, and lack of physics-grounded self-verification.", "We develop DelveAgent, a modular multi-agent framework with adaptive planning loop, dual-granularity memory, and hierarchical physics-grounded reflection.", "DelveAgent improves accuracy by up to 7.5 percentage points while reducing inference costs to approximately one-third of the strongest baseline."]
