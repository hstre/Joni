["Proposes a Correlation Aware Quantum Feature Map (CAQFM) which integrates feature dependencies into the quantum encoding process utilizing Pearson, Spearman, Kendall Tau, Mutual Information, and Distance Correlation measures, with dependencies exceeding a predefined threshold incorporated through controlled quantum gates.","Evaluated using a Variational Quantum Classifier on three benchmark datasets: breast cancer diagnosis, credit default prediction, and student placement classification.","Spearman and Kendall Tau based CAQFM variants achieved the highest predictive performance and consistently outperformed standard quantum feature maps, indicating that incorporating dependency information facilitates more discriminative quantum representations."]
